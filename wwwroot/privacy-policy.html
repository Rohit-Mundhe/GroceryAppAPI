Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Grocery Ordering App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Order Information: Items ordered, delivery address, and order history.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your grocery orders.
  • To authenticate your account securely.
  • To send order confirmations and updates.
  • To improve the app and user experience.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your orders (e.g., delivery partners) or as required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Passwords are stored using secure hashing algorithms and are never stored in plain text.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
